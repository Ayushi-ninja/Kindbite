How KindBite Works

AI Monitoring

Our system uses AI to detect and analyze surplus food in real time, ensuring nothing goes to waste.

IoT Integration

Smart sensors track food quantity and freshness, enabling timely pickups by nearby NGOs.

NGO Matching

We instantly match food donors with NGOs based on proximity and need, ensuring fast delivery.

How It Works

1. Donors Submit

Restaurants or individuals use the platform to register surplus food.

2. AI + IoT Analyze

Our system detects freshness, quantity, and recommends best matches instantly.

3. NGOs Receive & Deliver

Nearby NGOs are alerted to collect and redistribute food in real-time.

About KindBite

Our Mission

At KindBite, we aim to eliminate food waste and hunger by connecting food donors with local NGOs. Powered by AI and IoT, we make food redistribution smarter, faster, and more impactful.

Our Vision

A future where no food goes to waste and every meal finds a home. We envision a connected community where technology bridges the gap between abundance and need.
